## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Choosing Your Project

### Consider These Factors:

**Team Skills:**
- What technologies do you know?
- What do you want to learn?
- What are your strengths?

**Interest:**
- Choose something we're excited about
- Pick a domain you understand
- Think about what you'll enjoy building

**Scope:**
- Can you finish in the time available?
- Are the modules realistic?
- Do you have a backup plan?

**Uniqueness:**
- How can you make it stand out?
- What unique twist can you add?
- What custom modules could enhance it?
